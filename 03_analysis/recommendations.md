# Recommendations

## 1. Review Discount Strategy
Focus on discount levels at or above 30% where profitability declines.

## 2. Investigate Tables Sub-Category
Analyze pricing, cost, and discounting strategy for Tables.

## 3. Monitor Profit Alongside Sales
Avoid evaluating performance based on revenue alone.

## 4. Implement KPI Monitoring
Track profit, margin, discount level, and returns together.
